## What is Scalt?

Scalt is an **AI Agents as a Service** platform that allows you to automate your business processes with specialized intelligent agents, which we call **Scalters**.

Simple as a conversation. Powerful as code.

## How does it work?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe your need">
    Simply express what you want to automate with Scalt Assistant
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your Agent">
    Connect your tools, APIs and documents
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test in the Playground">
    Verify your Scalter's behavior before deployment
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch automation">
    Execute or schedule tasks with your Scalters
  </Step>
</Steps>
